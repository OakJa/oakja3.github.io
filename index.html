Communication Strategy

Most hearing aids are expensive and often sound too noisy. In daily conversations, users may struggle to hear clearly due to background noise. Our goal is to design affordable, clinically tested solutions that improve clarity and comfort for elderly users and patients with hearing difficulties.

Background and Justification

Hearing loss affects communication, independence, and quality of life. While commercial hearing aids exist, their high cost and noisy output limit adoption among elderly patients. A clinically guided approach can ensure better accessibility and user satisfaction.

Professional fitting and clinical validation are strongly recommended.
